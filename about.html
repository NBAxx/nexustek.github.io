About Us

Who We Are

NexusTek is a client-centered national managed IT services provider with over 25 years of experience serving small to midsized businesses. Our commitment to our customers guides everything we do—from the way we partner with our customers to help drive business outcomes, to how we handle support requests. As testament to our commitment to our customers, NexusTek has earned a 98% customer satisfaction rating. We are proud of our success and work every day to make sure that we earn our customers’ trust and respect by putting them at the center of everything we do.

What We Do

Trusted by thousands of small to midsized businesses, NexusTek offers a comprehensive portfolio comprised of end-user services, cloud, infrastructure, cybersecurity, and IT consulting. We provide holistic solutions for customers that deliver a superior end-user experience, backed by a 24/7/365 domestically staffed support team. We support customers not only to keep their IT running smoothly today, but also to harness technology to transform their business as their trusted technology partner.
